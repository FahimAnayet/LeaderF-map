*LeaderF-map*

INTRODUCTION~

This plugin is an extension of LeaderF, it is used to navigate the map.

==============================================================================
REQUIREMENTS~

- vim7.4.330 or higher.
- vim compiled with Python support, you can check by using `echo has('python')`
  or `echo has('python3')` to see if the result is 1.
==============================================================================
OPTIONS~

==============================================================================
USAGE~

:LeaderfMap                                   *LeaderfMap*
    Launch LeaderF to navigate the map.

==============================================================================
